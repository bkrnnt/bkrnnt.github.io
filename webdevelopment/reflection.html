Reflection

My name is Brooke Reutter, and I am a Communication major with an emphasis in Media at the University of Missouri. I am currently working as a Lead Videography Intern for Mizzou Women’s Basketball, with additional freelance experience in photography and videography. I created this resume website as both a professional resource and a creative portfolio to document my growth as a visual storyteller. This platform allows me to showcase my work, reflect on my experiences, and present my skills to future employers while expressing my creativity through visual media.

University of Missouri

Through my work with the University of Missouri’s athletic programs, I have gained hands-on experience shooting a wide variety of sports, including basketball, volleyball, softball, baseball, and golf. As a videography intern and now in a lead role with women’s basketball, I have worked within collaborative media teams to produce game content, social media features, behind-the-scenes footage, and interviews. These experiences have strengthened my creative problem-solving, attention to detail, adaptability, and ability to work efficiently in fast-paced environments while maintaining strong storytelling and visual consistency/branding.

Freelance

In addition to my collegiate sports experience, I have pursued freelance opportunities that have further expanded my creative perspective. I have worked in a variety of events including the Sophie Cunningham Class, OSG Regional Qualifier, Cattlemen’s Day Rodeo, concerts, weddings, engagements, and more. Working across different environments and scenarios has pushed me to expand my creative thinking and continuously seek new inspiration. These experiences have been instrumental in helping me develop a versatile approach to visual storytelling and build confidence as an independent creative.

Future Goals

Looking ahead, I hope to continue pursuing a career in sports media while growing my personal brand through freelance photography and videography. I am focused on developing my storytelling style, particularly within the sports context, and finding meaningful ways to capture emotion, energy, and detail in every project. I aim to collaborate with individuals and organizations that value creativity as a tool for engagement and impact. Through constant learning, experimentation, and inspiration, I am working toward a creative career that uses visual storytelling to help bring meaningful missions to life.
